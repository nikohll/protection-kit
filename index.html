Who This Kit Is For

Health insurance pays your doctors. Life Insurance with Living Benefits pays you.

A serious health crisis doesn't just hit your body. It hits your income, your business, your home, your family's lifestyle. Health insurance covers the medical bills. It does nothing for the rest. This kit is for the people who carry their own household and need to know the difference before life forces them to.

01

Single People

Your income is the engine of your entire life. Rent, bills, savings, retirement, all of it depends on you showing up. A serious health event interrupts that engine. Living Benefits give you cash to keep your life running while you heal.

02

Providers of the Household

You're the one your family counts on. The mortgage, childcare, groceries, school, all of it runs because you do. If your income gets interrupted, the household feels it within weeks. This kit shows you how to protect your family's lifestyle, not just their future.

Entrepreneurs

A health crisis can quietly interrupt your productivity and your revenue, even while the business expenses keep running. No employer benefits, no paid leave, no safety net. This kit shows you how to protect yourself, your family, and the business you built.
